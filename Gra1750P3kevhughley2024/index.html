Bubba Gump Shrimp Alfredo

Shrimp Alfredo Recipe

A creamy, flavorful pasta that is savory with every bit of shrimp, alfredo sauce, and spaghetti noodles.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Alfredo suace (of any kind)
  • Shrimp (any size raw or cooked)
  • Cheese (any kind)

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles
  2. While boiling the noodles, put any seasonsings on the shrimp if desired
  3. Once noodles are cooked thoroughly, add the alfredo sauce and proceed to cook the shrimp.
  4. While the shrimp is cooking, consistently stir the noodles with the sauce and put the cheese on it if desired.
  5. Finish cooking the shrimp then placed cooked shrimp into the same pot/pan as noodles and sauce. Continue to stir consistently so the food won't get stuck to the pan.
  6. Serve and enjoy every bite.
